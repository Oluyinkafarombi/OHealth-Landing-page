Ohealth

The Brain Health Initiative Nigeria (BHIN) is an initiative aimed at addressing key gaps in our understanding of how modifiable risk factors affect the brain health of Nigerian population. We aim to identify clusters of lifestyle, clinical and socioeconomic factors that promote brain health at different stages over the lifespan.
